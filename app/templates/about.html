Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate developers and system administrators, Hostify began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable web hosting that doesn't compromise on quality or support.

What started as a small team working out of a garage has grown into a leading web hosting provider serving over 50,000 customers across 120 countries. Our journey has been driven by one core belief - that every business, regardless of size, deserves enterprise-level hosting solutions.

Today, we operate 15 state-of-the-art data centers worldwide, maintain a 99.9% uptime guarantee, and continue to innovate in cloud hosting, security, and performance optimization.
